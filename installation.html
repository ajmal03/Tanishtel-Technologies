Installation

A smooth deployment, end to end

From site survey to go-live, our team handles every step so your institution is up and running quickly.

Setup requirements

A few essentials are needed at every installation point to guarantee reliable performance.

SIM cards

A working SIM card for each payphone deployed.

Strong mobile signal

Stable network coverage at the installation location.

Ventilation & visibility

Open, ventilated locations with clear visibility for safety.

Dedicated power

A reliable power supply for each phone unit.

Installation process

A predictable five-step rollout overseen by our engineers.

  1. 1

    Site assessment

    Our team visits your campus to identify ideal installation points.

  2. 2

    Provisioning

    SIMs, smart cards and devices are configured for your institution.

  3. 3

    Installation

    Mounting, wiring and power connections completed by trained engineers.

  4. 4

    Testing & training

    Signal tests, dashboard setup and warden training before go-live.

  5. 5

    Go-live & support

    System handed over with continuous monitoring and support.

Operational requirements

  • One SIM card per payphone unit
  • Strong mobile signal at every installation point
  • Adequate ventilation and visibility for safety
  • Dedicated power supply with surge protection
  • Designated administrator for the WebApp dashboard

Student guidelines

Handle calling cards carefully — the embedded chip is delicate. Avoid bending, scratching or exposing the card to moisture.

Use only at designated payphones.

Report lost cards immediately to the warden.

Stay within allowed call durations.
